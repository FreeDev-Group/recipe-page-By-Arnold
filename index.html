Simple Omelette Recipe

an easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs
cooked to perfection , optionaly filled with your choice ot cheese. vegetables or meats

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon butter or oil
  • Optioal fillings: cheese vegetables, cooked meants, herbs

Instructions

1.Beat the eggs: In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixed. You can add a tablespoon of water or milk for a fluter texture

2. Heat the pan: Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.

3. Cook the omelette: Once the butter is metted and bulling. pour in the eggs. tilt the pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface.

4. Add fillings (optional):When the eggs begin to set at the eggs but are still slightly runny in the middle. spinkle your chosen over one halt of the omelette.

5. Folg abd serve: As the omelette continious to cook , carefully lift one edge and fold it over the fillinds. let it cook for another minute, then slide it into a plate.

6.Serve hat, with additional salt and pepper if needed.
